MEMORABLE MOMENTS WITH HER GRANDPARENTS:-

she was the first grandchildren of her grandparents(thammi,didan,dadu),so naturally after seeing her appearance
all of them were as much happy they had never been,and since then her existance provides a feeling of pleasure
to them

THE DAY SHE BORN:-

It was the morning of winter,everyboy from both families including me was present in the hospital,on that day the
GOD GOFTED girl first saw the light of this beautifull world,everyone among us was happy,some of them went
closure to her,touching her cute small hands and blessing her for the battle of life

HER FIRST 7 DAYS:-

For first 1-2 days she was on hospital along with her mother,relatives from both the families visited then
after the duration of a specific time period,i also went one day to see her but she was so tired and sleeping then

THE DAY WHEN HER NAME WAS DECIDED:-

As usual everyone has given their chosen name name for the baby girl and then according to the bengali rituals
every name was put into a lamp and the brighter&long lasting one will be selected as her name.In this way the name
"DHRUVIKA" was selected as her name for the rest of her life

MEANING OF HER NAME:-

After a long discussion among all her parents and relatives her final name was decided as "DHRUVIKA",meaning of
this name is firmly fixed
